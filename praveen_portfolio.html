The System

Three-layer automation pipeline

Built specifically for a digital nomad influencer across Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube, and Threads.

01
Voice Intelligence Layer
Python · Pinecone · CrewAI Analyst Agent
Ingests your existing Instagram captions and LinkedIn posts, extracts your tone, vocabulary, hook structure, signature phrases and content patterns. Builds a living Voice DNA document that gets injected into every generation prompt. Updated weekly as you post more.
02
Multi-Agent Content Generation
CrewAI · LangGraph · RAG over post embeddings
LangGraph orchestrates four specialist agents: caption writer, hashtag researcher, hook variant generator (3 A/B options), and CTA optimizer. A brand QA agent checks every draft. A humanisation agent removes AI tells and adds your natural rhythm before anything reaches review.
03
Distribution & Feedback Loop
n8n · Platform APIs · Analytics Aggregator
You review via Slack. She approves via Telegram/WhatsApp — one tap. n8n auto-schedules and posts across all 5 platforms. Analytics flow back into the trend brief weekly, so the system learns what performs and improves every cycle.
